About EduMind Nexus

EduMind Nexus AI is a cutting-edge, multimodal intelligent study assistant designed to revolutionize how students learn. Powered by DeepSeek's advanced AI, it offers features like:

  • 🤖 Intelligent AI Chatbot for instant doubt solving
  • 📄 PDF/DOCX Upload & Smart Summarization
  • 📝 AI-Powered Quiz Generator with Auto-Grading
  • 📅 Personalized Study Planner with AI recommendations
  • 🎴 Interactive Flashcards for effective revision
  • 🎤 Voice Input & Text-to-Speech for hands-free learning
  • 📊 Analytics Dashboard to track progress

This project represents my final year endeavor to combine the power of artificial intelligence with educational technology, creating a tool that is both powerful and accessible. Built with ❤️ for learners everywhere.
